Kaosland Cup 2022 Event

ELIGIBILITY

The Event is open to any individual who is 18 years of age or older.  It is void in countries where prohibited by law. Participation in the Event is subject to these rules (the “Official Rules”).

EVENT PERIOD

Kaosland Cup NFT Whitelist Registration 

The Kaosland Cup Whitelist registration begins at 12:00 a.m. Universal Time (“UTC”) on November 5th, 2022 and ends at 11:59 p.m. UTC on November 19th, 2022 (the “Whitelist Registration Period”).

Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition 

The start of The Event will be announced via Twitter no later than Nov 25th and it will end at 11:59 p.m. UTC on December 12th and, with the condition that there are at least (5,000) five thousand entries submitted. (the “Selfie Comeptition Event Period)

Kaosland Cup Shared Pool Airdrop

Kaosland Cup Airdrop event will only be activated when the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition receives 5,000 entries. If 5,000 entries are not achieved BC reserves the right to modify or cancel the Kaosland Cup Airdrop.

If 5,000 entries are achieved in the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition then the Kaosland Cup Airdrop will commence. The Event begins at 12:00 a.m. Universal Time (“UTC”) on December 14th, 2022 and ends at 11:59 p.m. UTC on December 18th, 2022 (the “Airdrop Event Period”).

DISTRIBUTION PERIOD

Kaosland Cup NFT Whitelist Spots

Due to the high volume of participants in the Whitelist event, it may take BC until 11:59 p.m. UTC on December 5, 2022 for BC (the “Whitelist Distribution Period”) to distribute the rewards to all winning entrants.  

December 6th, 2022 Update: After technical consideration and a high volume of entries the team has decided to mint all of the Kaosland Cup NFTs in a single batch. For this reason, the distribution for the Kaosland Cup NFT Whitelist Spots will happen at the same date and time as the distribution for the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition.  

Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition 

Due to the high volume of participants in the Event, it may take BC until 11:59 p.m. UTC on December 13th, 2022 for BC (the “Selfie Competition Distribution Period”) to distribute the rewards to all winning entrants.  

Kaosland Cup Airdrop

Due to the high volume of participants in the Event, it may take BC until 11:59 p.m. UTC on December 22nd, 2022 for BC (the “Airdrop Distribution Period”) to distribute the rewards to all winning entrants.  

PRIZES

Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022

The Kaosland Cup Airdrop prize pool is up to 500,000 NEER will be awarded during the Airdrop Distribution Period, but the date and time will otherwise be determined by BC at its sole discretion. Each prize will be deposited to the winning Entrants’ crypto wallet by BC.  

Kaosland Cup NFT 

To be eligible to win the Kaosland Cup Airdrop prize the Entrant must hold a Kaosland Cup NFT. A total of 5000 Kaosland NFTs will be minted as distributed to Entrants that; 

    • Win a Kaosland Cup NFT Whitelist Spot

    Up to 1,000 Whitelist spots for the Kaosland Cup NFT will be awarded randomly to Entrants that successfully complete the registration process. The deposit of the Kaosland Cup NFTs will be awarded during the Whitelist Distribution Period, but the date and time will otherwise be determined by BC at its sole discretion.

    • Win the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition 

    Up to 3730 Kaosland Cup NFTs will be awarded to Entrants that successfully complete the entry process for the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition. The distribution of the Kaosland Cup NFTs will be started during the Selfie Distribution Period, but the date and time will otherwise be determined by BC at its sole discretion.

    • Are an Evangelist

    Up to 21 Whitelist spots will be given to Bit.Country and Metaverse.Network Evangelists. The distribution of the Kaosland Cup NFTs will be awarded during the Whitelist Distribution Period, but the date and time will otherwise be determined by BC at its sole discretion.

    • Are a Bit.Country & Metaverse.Network partners

    Up to 200 Whitelist spots will be awarded randomly to Bit.Country and Metaverse.Network partners that sign up for the Whitelist event. The distribution of the Kaosland Cup NFTs will be awarded during the Whitelist Distribution Period, but the date and time will otherwise be determined by BC at its sole discretion.

    • Raw land Block Owner

    50 Whitelist spots will be awarded to metaverse owners that successfully purchased a raw Land Block in Bit.Country Xenoform event. 

    Holders of the Kaosland Cup NFT will receive: 

    • 1 Complete set of Bit.Country Kaosland Cup avatar wearables (3 Individual NFTS). 
      • 1 x Shirt 
      • 1 x Shorts
      • 1 x Shoes and Socks
    • The opportunity to be eligible for the Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022.

    If the advertised prize cannot be awarded for any reason, BC reserves the right to substitute a prize of equal or greater value. All federal, state and local income taxes on the prizes and all other costs associated with acceptance or use of the prizes are the sole responsibility of the winners.

    HOW TO ENTER

    To participate in the Event, you will need a Twitter account. Creating a Twitter account is free of charge.  

    To obtain a Twitter account, visit www.Twitter.com and follow the instructions to create an account.  By submitting your information and creating a Twitter account, you will be required to agree to the Twitter user agreement.  

    How to Enter to Win a Kaosland Cup NFT

      Register for the Kaosland Cup NFT Whitelist Spot

      To be eligible to win Kaosland Cup NFT Whitelist spots you must complete the following collectively, an “Entry”: 

      • Complete the registration form.
      • Select which team you would like to support in the Kaosland Cup Competition.
      • Accept the Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022 Terms and Conditions. 
      • Submit your application on-chain to verify the wallet provided is the same wallet provided in the form. (To submit the application on-chain you will need at least ~0.13 $NEER to pay the transaction fees on Metaverse.Network Pioneer).

      Evangelists

      For a Bit.Country and Metaverse.Network Evangelists to be eligible to receive a Kaosland Cup NFT you must complete the following collectively, an “Entry”: 

      • Select which team you would like to support in the Kaosland Cup Competition.
      • Accept the Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022 Terms and Conditions. 
      • Submit your application on-chain to verify the wallet provided is the same wallet provided in the form. (To submit the application on-chain you will need at least 0.02 $NEER to pay the transaction fees on Metaverse.Network Pioneer).

      Bit.Country & Metaverse.Network Partner

      For a Bit.Country and Metaverse.Network partner to be eligible to win a Kaosland Cup NFT you must complete the following collectively, an “Entry”: 

      • Complete the registration form with a partner referral code.
      • Select which team you would like to support in the Kaosland Cup Competition.
      • Accept the Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022 Terms and Conditions. 
      • Submit your application on-chain to verify the wallet provided is the same wallet provided in the form. (To submit the application on-chain you will need at least 0.02 $NEER to pay the transaction fees on Metaverse.Network Pioneer).

      Raw Land Block Owner

      For a Bit.Country and Metaverse.Network Raw Land Block owner to be eligible to win a Kaosland Cup NFT you must complete the following collectively, an “Entry”: 

      • Purchased a Raw Land Block in the Xenoform event. 
      • Select which team you would like to support in the Kaosland Cup Competition.
      • Accept the Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022 Terms and Conditions. 
      • Submit your application on-chain to verify the wallet provided is the same wallet provided in the form. (To submit the application on-chain you will need at least 0.02 $NEER to pay the transaction fees on Metaverse.Network Pioneer).

      Enter Kaosland Selfie Competition

      See details in ‘How to Enter the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition’ below.

      Entries made in any other manner will not be eligible for the Event.  

      How to Enter the Kaosland Cup Selfie Competition 

        To be eligible to win the Kaosland Cup Selfie Challenge an Entrant must accumulate at least 90% of the total points available. There are 6 tasks, that the Entrant can complete. Each task is allocated a set number of points. When an Entrant collects at least 90% of the total points it will collectively be considered as an “Entry”:

        Task List: 

        • Take a selfie or video recording of your avatar in your selected your favourite team wearables in either the Kaosland Stadium or MetaDojo_io  Stadium. 
        • Share the selfie or video recording on Twitter with the following quote: ‘ Hey ho, let's go…  I back “Selected Country” to win the #WorldCup2022. 

        Check out my #KaoslandCup2022 selfie! 

        Join @BitDotCountry competition to be in to win a share of 500,000 $NEER”

        • Follow @BitDotCountry on Twitter
        • Follow @MetaDojo_IO on Twitter
        • Retweet the @BitDotCountry Twitter Kaosland Cup Competition announcement post.  
        • Retweet the @MetaDojo_IO Twitter Kaosland Cup Competition announcement post.  

        An Entrant will receive points to boost the chance of winning if they: 

        • Registered but missed out on a Whitelist spot
        • Participated in pioneer.bit.country 
        • Participated in testnet.bit.country 
        • Share a selfie or video recording on Twitter that includes; 
          • Avatar Emotes
          • Modification of the Kaosland or MetaDojo_IO stadium Sandpit with voxels, props or 3D assets.

        Entries made in any other manner will not be eligible for the Event.  

        Entries must accept the Kaosland Cup Airdrop 2022 Terms and Conditions.
